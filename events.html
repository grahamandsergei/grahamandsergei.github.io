Main Ceremony & Drinks

2:00 PM 4:30 PM
Sunday 17 September, 2023

Hand-Fasting Ceremony in Breaffy House Hotel followed by drinks. Hand-fasting is a unity ritual in which a couple stands face to face as their hands are tied together, hence, tying the knot! As their hands are bound, words are recited that express their commitment to each another. This ritual is done within the context of a Celtic Pagan ceremony which will be conducted by a Druid. If this offends anybody's personal religious beliefs, we will not take non-attendance of this ceremony personally.
